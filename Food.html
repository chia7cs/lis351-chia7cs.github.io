My favourite food is Nasi Lemak. Nasi lemak is a rice dish commonly served with roasted nuts, egg, ikan bilis (anchovies), and slices of cucumber. Literally meaning “fatty rice” in Malay, nasi lemak's distinctive taste comes from cooking the rice in coconut milk and pandan leaves which gives the dish its rich flavour and fragrant aroma.

Important Ingredient List:
Anchovies
We fry anchovies to add the special aroma to the dish
Sambal
Sambal needs at least 4 hours to cooka. It consists of a paste of red or green chiles ground together with any number of other ingredients: garlic, shallots, lemongrass, galangal, tomatoes, and/or shrimp paste.
